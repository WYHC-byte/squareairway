About SquareAirway

SquareAirway is an innovative virtual aviation project that brings the excitement of flight simulation to a dynamic, engaging platform. We're passionate about creating immersive experiences that let aviation enthusiasts explore, learn, and connect.

Whether you're a casual player or a serious aviation fan, SquareAirway offers something for everyone.

Our Mission

  • Create realistic flight experiences
  • Build a global aviation community
  • Promote aviation education and passion
